Cocktail name: Margarita
Cocktail name: Margarita | Alcohol percentage: 20% | Ingredients: 1 tablespoon kosher salt, 1.5 fluid ounces tequila, 1 fluid ounce orange flavored liqueur (such as Cointreau), 1/2 fluid ounce lime juice, 1 cup ice, 1 lime wheel.
Cocktail name: Pina Colada
Cocktail name: Pina Colada | Alcohol percentage: 13% | Ingredients: Rum, Cream of coconut (not coconut cream), Pineapple juice, Ice, Frozen pineapple, Lime juice.
Cocktail name: Mojito
Cocktail name: Mojito | Alcohol percentage: 13% | Ingredients: 5 mint leaves, 2 ounces white rum, 1 ounce fresh lime juice, 1/2 ounce simple syrup, Ice, Club soda or sparkling water, Lime slices.
